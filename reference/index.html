How do I get a medical marijuana card online?
Complete a secure intake form, then meet a licensed physician over video or audio. If you qualify, your certification is emailed to you — finish any state registration and you're set to buy legally.
Can I really get approved the same day?
Yes — same-day evaluations are standard. Most qualified patients receive their recommendation within minutes to an hour after the consult, depending on state processing.
How much does it cost?
Evaluations range from $55–$199 depending on your state, with no hidden charges. And if the doctor doesn't approve you, you pay nothing — guaranteed.
Do I need to visit a clinic in person?
No. The entire process — intake, doctor consultation, and certification — happens online from home. No waiting rooms, ever.
Is my information private?
Completely. Your personal and medical data is protected by HIPAA-compliant systems end-to-end. Consultations are confidential and never shared.
How long is my card valid?
Typically one year, though it varies by state. When it's time, a quick online renewal consult keeps your access uninterrupted.
What if I'm not approved?
You get a 100% refund automatically. You only ever pay for a successful certification — that's our promise.
